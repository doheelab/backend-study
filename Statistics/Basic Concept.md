## Poulation vs Sample

- Population (σ)

- Sample (S): subset of population

  - representative of the population

  - large sample

## Measure of Central Tendency

- Mean

  - population mean

  - sample mean

  - geometric mean

- Median

  - percentile (하위 비율): percentage of your data that falls below a certain number

  - quantile (하위 %)

- Mode (most often)

## Variance & Standard Deviation

- Variance

- Standard Deviation

  - -σ to σ: 68%

  - -2σ to 3σ: 95%

  - -3σ to 3σ: 99%
